About the game

On the home page, You can see how much score you have gained over time. What your status is based on how much score you gained and how much score you need to upgrade your status


To start the game click play and you will be able to create a username.

After you create a username you can then go ahead and choose a difficulty. There are two difficulties in the game.


1. Normal Difficullty

Normal difficulty contains 5 questions in each game and each correct question gives 20 points out of 100 total points. each question should be answered within 10 seconds at the end of the game you will be presented with a feedback on how you did. You can then choose to play again or if you feel confident, you can go harder and play next level or you can Go back to main menu to see your total score and status


2. Hard difficulty

hard difficulty contains 10 questions in each game and each correct question gives 40 points out of 400 total points. Each question should be answered within 10 seconds. at the end of the game you will be presented with a feedback on how you did. You can then choose to play again or Go back to main menu to see your total score and status
